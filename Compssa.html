COMPSSA

Welcome to the Computer Science Students Association (COMPSSA)! As a vibrant community of passionate learners and innovators, CSSA serves as the hub for all things related to computer science at University of Ghana. Committed to fostering collaboration, skill development, and networking opportunities, we organize engaging events, workshops, and industry connections to empower our members in their academic and professional journeys. Join us to explore the ever-evolving world of technology, share knowledge, and connect with like-minded peers. Together, let's code, create, and inspire!
