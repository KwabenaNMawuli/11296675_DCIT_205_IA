Graduate programmes

Embrace the cutting-edge graduate education our department offers to join the world's top 1% in tech

Among the graduate programmes our department offers are MSc Computer Science, MPhil Computer Science and PhD Computer science degree.

Our postgraduate degrees help usher you into a the highest echelons of mastery in the field of technology.

MSc Computer Science

The MSc in Computer Science program offers an advanced level of study in various aspects of computer science, aiming to deepen students' understanding and expertise in the field.
The program often covers topics such as algorithms, data structures, software engineering, artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer networks and cybersecurity.

MPhil Computer Science

MPhil in Computer Science is an advanced postgraduate degree program focused on research within the field of computer science. It involves an in-depth exploration of specific areas of computer science through research, coursework, and the completion of a thesis. Students pursuing an MPhil in Computer Science delve into advanced topics in computing, which can include but are not limited to: Research methodologies in computer science. Advanced algorithms and data structures. Machine learning, artificial intelligence, and data science. Computer networks, cybersecurity, and information security. Software engineering and development methodologies. Advanced topics in computer architecture or computer systems. The program often allows students to work closely with faculty members or supervisors on research projects, enabling them to contribute new insights or advancements to the field. It aims to cultivate critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and a deeper understanding of theoretical and practical aspects of computer science.

PhD Computer Science

A Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Computer Science is the highest academic degree offered by the department, focused on advanced research, innovation, and the creation of new knowledge within computer science.Students undertake extensive original research in a specialized area of computer science. This research often leads to the creation of a doctoral thesis, which contributes novel insights or advancements to the field.While coursework requirements vary, students usually take advanced classes in computer science theory, research methodologies, and specialized topics relevant to their area of interest. PhD candidates work closely with faculty advisors and other researchers, engaging in collaborations, seminars, and discussions to refine their research ideas and methodologies.
